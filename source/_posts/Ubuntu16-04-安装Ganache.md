### Ganache简介
构建可视化的区块链私有网络

### 下载和安装
```
wget https://github.com/trufflesuite/ganache/releases/download/v1.0.1/ganache-1.0.1-x86_64.AppImage  //下载ganache
chmod +x ganache-1.0.1-x86_64.AppImage //修改权限
sudo ./ganache-1.0.1-x86_64.AppImage //启动ganache
```

### 启动

```
sudo ./ganache-1.0.1-x86_64.AppImage //启动ganache
```
